How to Get Here

Sangli is well-connected by road and rail. Here are some options to reach Sangli:

  • By Train: The nearest railway station is Sangli Junction.
  • By Bus: Regular bus services operate from major cities.
  • By Car: You can drive via NH-48 for a scenic route.
